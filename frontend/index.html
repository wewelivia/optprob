Positioning summary

Conviction confluence of three corroborating signals per strike: open-interest build (ΔOI), turnover intensity (volume/OI), and pricing confirmation (ΔIV) — each z-scored against its own recent volatility so a quiet-market move isn't mistaken for conviction. A strike scores high only when magnitude is large and ≥2 of 3 signals agree in direction; strong-but-opposed signals are flagged conflicting, never averaged away.
